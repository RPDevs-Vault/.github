# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 26, 2026

## 1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy governs your use of any software applications, scripts, configurations, managers, or related services (collectively, the "Services") provided by RPDevs-Vault or Esoteric Root Collective ("we," "us," or "our"). 

Our privacy philosophy is absolute: **what is yours remains yours.** We build our Services to respect your privacy by design and by default. 

## 2. Information We Do Not Collect
To be entirely clear, our Services are designed to operate locally on your device or within your own infrastructure. We do not automatically collect, harvest, scrape, transmit, or store any of the following:
* **Personal Information:** We do not collect names, email addresses, IP addresses, browsing history, or device identifiers.
* **Telemetry and Analytics:** We do not track how you use the Services, which features you interact with, or how often you use them.
* **Debug and Crash Logs:** We do not automatically gather error reports, crash logs, or performance metrics.

**Zero data leaves your device through our Services without your explicit, manual intervention.**

## 3. Information You Manually Submit
The absolute *only* time we ever receive any information from you is if you deliberately and manually choose to send it to us. 

If you encounter an issue and choose to contact us for support, report a bug, or submit a feature request (e.g., via GitHub Issues, pull requests, or email), you may be sharing information with us voluntarily. This may include:
* Your email address, username, or alias.
* The contents of your message and description of your issue.
* Any debug logs, screenshots, configuration files, or system information you explicitly attach and submit.

**How we use manually submitted data:**
Any information you manually send to us is used exclusively for the purpose you sent it—specifically, to troubleshoot your issue, reply to your inquiry, or improve the Services based on your feedback. Publicly submitted data (such as a GitHub Issue) remains public as per the platform's standard operations.

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure
Because we do not automatically collect any data, we have nothing to sell, rent, or share with third parties. Any data you manually submit to us privately for support purposes will never be sold, used for marketing, or shared outside of our organization, except as explicitly required by valid legal processes.

This Privacy Policy only covers the software and services provided directly by us. We do not control, and are not responsible for, the privacy practices or data collection policies of any third-party services you choose to interact with while using our Services.
